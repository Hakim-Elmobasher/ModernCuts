SERVICES CONTACT US
How do I book an appointment?
You can book an appointment by visiting our website and clicking on the "BOOK NOW" button. Follow the instructions to select your preferred services, date, and time.
What services do you offer?
We offer a wide range of services including haircuts, styling, beard grooming, hair coloring, and various hair treatments. You can find detailed information about our services on the Services page.
How can I cancel or reschedule my appointment?
To cancel or reschedule your appointment, please log in to your account on our website and go to the Appointments section. There you can find options to modify or cancel your existing appointments.
What safety measures are you taking in light of COVID-19?
We are following all recommended guidelines and safety protocols to ensure the well-being of our customers and staff. This includes regular sanitization, social distancing, and the use of personal protective equipment.
Do you offer group discounts for events like weddings?
Yes, we offer special group discounts for events such as weddings and other special occasions. Please contact us directly to discuss the details of your event and the services you require.
How experienced are your stylists and barbers?
Our stylists and barbers are highly experienced professionals with years of expertise in the industry. They undergo regular training to stay updated with the latest trends and techniques, ensuring you receive top-notch services.
Can I bring my own hair care products?
Yes, you are welcome to bring your own hair care products if you have specific preferences or allergies. However, our stylists also use high-quality, professional products to ensure the best results.
How long does a typical haircut appointment take?
A typical haircut appointment usually takes around 30 to 45 minutes, depending on the complexity of the style and your preferences. However, for more elaborate services, the duration may vary. Feel free to ask your stylist for a more accurate estimate based on your requirements.
